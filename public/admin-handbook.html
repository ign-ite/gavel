Gavel Admin Handbook

Admins should approve only listings that are accurate, safe, reviewable, and clearly supported by media and seller disclosures.

Approval checklist

  1. The images match the product and are usable.
  2. The product contains no sexual or explicit content.
  3. The product contains no violent or harmful content.
  4. The category, title, and claims are accurate.
  5. The description and specs match the uploaded media.

Reject when

  • Media is misleading, unrelated, rotated badly, or too poor to verify.
  • Important defects, missing parts, or authenticity issues are hidden.
  • The listing looks fraudulent, unsafe, or intentionally miscategorized.
  • The seller has not provided enough information to review honestly.
