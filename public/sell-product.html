Seller Submission Tips

Give the reviewer exactly what they need to approve the listing in one pass.

  • State the current condition clearly, including scratches, defects, repairs, and missing parts.
  • Add ownership markers such as serial numbers, invoice references, or packaging details.
  • Mention what is included: box, charger, papers, warranty card, accessories, or certificates.
  • Make sure images are upright and the video shows a full walkaround without heavy blur.
