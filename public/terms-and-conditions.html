Marketplace policy

Terms and Conditions

These rules govern listing, bidding, messaging, meetups, moderation, and wallet-backed auction activity on Gavel.

Seller responsibilities

  • You must own the item or have permission to sell it.
  • Your images, video, category, specs, and description must represent the real product accurately.
  • You must disclose damage, missing parts, authenticity issues, and included accessories.
  • Admins can reject, pause, or remove misleading, unsafe, prohibited, or incomplete listings.

Buyer and bidding rules

  • Bids are binding once confirmed.
  • Auto-bid uses your chosen maximum and only charges you if you win.
  • Anti-snipe time extensions, reserve handling, and closing rules are enforced by the platform.
  • Outbid notices, chat records, and bid history are part of the transaction record.

Messaging and meetups

  • Use chat for product questions, more photos, meetup planning, or post-auction coordination.
  • Harassment, spam, impersonation, fraud, and abusive off-platform behavior are prohibited.
  • Campus meetups should happen in safe public locations.

Moderation and enforcement

  • Admins and super admins review listings using the moderation checklist and handbook standards.
  • Rejected listings must include a reason. Approved listings must pass every approval checklist item.
  • Accounts or listings may be limited or removed where there is abuse, fraud, or safety risk.
