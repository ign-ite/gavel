Campus Marketplace Flow

What Gavel is and exactly how it works

Gavel is a campus-first marketplace for auctions and direct product discovery. Students list products, admins verify them, buyers discover them through explore reels or the auction grid, then chat and complete the handoff on campus.

Platform loop

Sell -> Review -> Discover -> Bid or chat -> Win -> Meet on campus -> Review each other.

Seller uploads images and video
Admin verifies media and claims
Buyers watch reels or open detail page
Bids, auto-bids, and chat stay attached to the product

Selling on Gavel

1

Upload your listing

Add clear images, one verification video, price, description, condition, and specs. Your draft is saved so you can come back without losing progress.

2

Admin review

Admins verify that the images match the product, the content is safe, the category is correct, and the description is honest. Approvals need a completed checklist. Rejections require a written note.

3

Go live and track performance

Once approved, your product appears in explore, auctions, and personalized sections. You can track views and relist closed items.

Buying and bidding

1

Discover products

Browse live auctions in the grid or scroll reels in Explore. Videos stay muted and properly oriented so you can focus on the product.

2

Open the product detail page

Use the media carousel, zoom on images, review description and specs, then place a bid or set a maximum auto-bid.

3

Message the seller

Every chat thread is grouped by product, so you can ask for more photos, negotiate details, and schedule the campus meetup after the auction closes.
